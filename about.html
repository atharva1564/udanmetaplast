About UDAN Metaplast

Excellence in Precision Mould Manufacturing

Our Story

Founded with a vision to deliver excellence in precision mould manufacturing, UDAN Metaplast has grown from a small workshop to a leading manufacturer serving the automotive and luggage industries across India.

Our journey began with a simple mission: to create moulds that not only meet but exceed industry standards. Today, we are proud to be trusted partners for major automotive and luggage manufacturers, delivering quality and precision in every project.

With state-of-the-art machinery and a team of skilled professionals, we continue to innovate and push the boundaries of what's possible in mould manufacturing.

What We Offer

Comprehensive mould manufacturing solutions tailored to your needs

Custom Design

Our engineering team works closely with clients to design moulds that perfectly match their specifications and requirements.

Precision Manufacturing

Utilizing advanced machinery including Wire Cut, VMC, EDM, and Grinding machines for exacting precision.

Quality Assurance

Rigorous testing and quality control processes ensure every mould meets the highest industry standards.

Timely Delivery

We understand the importance of deadlines and ensure on-time delivery of all projects without compromising quality.

Technical Support

Comprehensive technical support and consultation throughout the project lifecycle and beyond.

Maintenance Services

Ongoing maintenance and repair services to ensure optimal performance of your moulds.
